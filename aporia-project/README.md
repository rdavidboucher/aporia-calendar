APORIA — The Applied Philosophy Daily Companion"The examination is not finished. The examination is what the examined life is. Go on."Aporia is a 365-day curriculum and software architecture designed for the continuous practice of the examined life. It is built on a content-first philosophy, where perennial wisdom (The Seed) is synthesized into actionable psychological frameworks and personal journaling.I. THE 12-ARC CURRICULUMThe year is organized into 12 thematic Arcs, moving from internal perception to terminal questions.ArcThemePrimary Thinkers01The Foundation of AttentionAurelius, Newport, Suzuki02The Architecture of SelfKierkegaard, Sartre, James03The World As It IsKahneman, Taleb, Pascal04Making ThingsVan Gogh, Rilke, Pressfield05What We Owe Each OtherAristotle, Adam Smith, Levinas06The Management of FortuneBoethius, Montaigne, Seneca07The Body and Timede Beauvoir, Merleau-Ponty, Sacks08What Does It Mean to Create?Arendt, Heidegger, Bachelard09Society and PowerBaldwin, Rawls, Gramsci10What Remains in the Dark?Weil, Frankl, Beckett11What Does It Mean to Love?Plato, Murdoch, Barthes12What Does It Mean to Face Death?Montaigne, Epicurus, Kübler-RossII. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTUREThe Daily Loop (The Four Modules)The Main Entry: New curriculum material (Seed, Synthesis, Application).The SRS Review: 3–5 items from previous days, resurfaced via the SM-2 Algorithm to combat the forgetting curve.The Reflection Prompt: Rotating journal archetypes:Synthetic: Analyzing the new material.Audit: Reflecting on past applications.In-The-Wild: Applying models to real-time events.The Archive: All entries are tagged and saved for Arc 12, where your own history becomes the primary text.Stack RequirementsFrontend: React (Vite)Backend: Firebase Firestore (Local-first with Cloud Sync)Design: High-typography, minimalist CSS (serif-first).Data Format: JSON generated from .txt source files via parser.py.III. CORE POLICIESQuote Policy: All quotes must be precisely sourced. Use (paraphrase) or (source uncertain) where verification is impossible. Prioritize primary correspondence (Letters).Evidence Policy: Flag empirical support with standard qualifiers: [Strong alignment], [Good directional support], or [Speculative application].Tagging System: Every journal response must be tagged using the canonical set ([practice], [mindset], [warning], etc.) to ensure a coherent end-of-year synthesis.V. THE TERMINAL GOALThe app is not a "summary" of books. It is a vehicle designed to ensure that the books you have read actually change how you inhabit your own life. The goal is the somehow-on: the irreducible persistence of the examined life.
